Data Deletion

Contact: privacy@astrosimple.app

You can permanently delete your Astrosimple account and all associated data at any time. Deletion removes your account, sign-in identity, stored birth profiles, generated charts and interpretations, chat history, and subscription records from our servers.

Option 1: Delete from the app

  1. Open Astrosimple and go to the Settings tab.
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email privacy@astrosimple.app from the address associated with your account and request deletion. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by reply.

Signed in with Facebook, Google, Apple, or X?

Deleting your Astrosimple account removes the data we received from your sign-in provider (name, email, avatar) along with everything else. You may additionally revoke Astrosimple's access in your provider's account settings — for Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.

What is not deleted

Your subscription is managed by Apple. Deleting your account does not cancel an active App Store subscription — manage it in your Apple ID settings. Anonymized error reports already sent to our crash-monitoring service expire automatically per its retention policy.
